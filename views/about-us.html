Our story

i-medify AG was founded in 2011 with the goal to develop professional software in the pharma industrie.
Since 2013 i-medify provides a product called mediproc which supports the entire blistering process.

i-medify AG was founded in order to develop software for specific production processes in the pharmaceutical industry. This initial focus is broadened to other areas of application, especially in the field of logistics and planning. To implement these projects we cooperate with international software companies, with whom we have been cultivating long-standing relations.

We develop high quality software to optimize the quality of care and the safety of medicines for the (Swiss) healthcare.
Therefore, optimal provisioning of patients with medications and improved efficiency in the care costs and increased safety in the medication is ensured.
